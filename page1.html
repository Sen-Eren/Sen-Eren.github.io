Fully Digital Enterprise

What is 'Fully Digital Enterprise'?

A fully digital enterprise is a company that uses digital technology in all aspects of its business, from interacting with customers to developing new products and services.


What are the cyber Security challenges/concerns with a fully digital enterprise?

Fully digital enterprises are more vulnerable to cyber attacks because they have more digital assets and touchpoints. They are also more complex and interconnected, which can make it difficult to identify and secure all of their assets.


What are the cyber security challenges for a bricks and mortar SME wanting to become a digital enterprise?

Bricks and mortar SMEs that are transitioning to digital enterprises face additional cyber security challenges, such as limited resources, lack of expertise, and legacy systems.


Do you agree with the views expressed, especially in light of the 'energy crisis' experienced worldwide in 2022?

Yes, I agree with the views expressed. The energy crisis has highlighted the importance of cyber security for businesses of all sizes, especially those that are reliant on digital technologies.

IoT Seminar

What is IoT

Think of it like a giant network of connected devices, all talking to each other and sharing information. This allows us to collect data about the world around us, understand patterns, and make informed decisions.
Here's a simple example to illustrate the concept:
You have a smart water bottle that connects to your phone.
The bottle has a sensor that measures the amount of water you drink.
The bottle sends this data to your phone, which can track your daily water intake.
You can set goals for yourself and receive reminders to stay hydrated.
This is just a basic example, but it shows how IoT can be used to make our lives easier, more efficient, and healthier.
